Abstract

This website aims to model all the confirmed moonquakes on a 3D model of the moon. Since the arrival of Apollo 11 passing by Apollo 12 ,14,15 till Apollo 16.Where there was recorded 13,000 reading about quakes and after filtering and cleaning reached 7,082 reading only. Using these reading to analyze 166 on the moon but since 60 of them were corrupted you can only find 106 on the website
